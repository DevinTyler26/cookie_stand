Salmon Cookies is a cookie shop overlooking the Elliott Bay waterfront in Seattle, Washington, United States. The cookie shop opened August 17, 1907, by Pat Salmon's great grand father, Pat Salmon, and is one of the oldest continuously operated Salmon Cookie Shop in the United States.
